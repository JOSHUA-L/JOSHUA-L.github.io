About My Family

My family is held together on the principles of love, faith, co-operation, kindness, and much beyond that. We are united through blood and mind and have been this way for years. I stand to say well of them now because of their contributions to my life and mine to theirs. I know this because of the true fact that we all will be dead if this wasn't to be. Of course, you may say "what makes your family unique?" and instead of using show-off GIFs like my last HTML/CSS project, I am going to tell the story of them starting from the year 2004, as long as I've witnessed, or in my sister's case, from 2011 onwards.
